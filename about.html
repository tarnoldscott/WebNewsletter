About the web team

We build, maintain, and evolve plaid.com — the front door to every product, campaign, and narrative Plaid puts into the world.

What we do

The web team owns plaid.com end-to-end: strategy, design, engineering, content, experimentation, and performance. We partner across marketing, product marketing, brand, demand gen, and engineering to ship customer-facing surfaces that drive pipeline, educate buyers, and tell Plaid's story.

We think of the website as a product — not a brochure. That means we measure it, experiment on it, and treat every page as a growth lever.

Our pillars
Growth & conversion
Experimentation, CRO, and page-level performance to turn traffic into pipeline.
Storytelling
Use case, industry, and product narratives that meet buyers where they are.
Platform
CMS, infrastructure, tooling, and developer experience that let us ship fast.
Design systems
Reusable components and patterns that keep the site consistent and scalable.

About this newsletter

The Web We Weave is a monthly digest that rounds up what shipped, what was learned, and what's coming on plaid.com. It's written for anyone at Plaid who cares about the website — which should be everyone.

Each issue covers the prior month's launches, experiments, platform work, and shoutouts. Got something to add? Drop it in #website-open.
