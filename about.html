About the web team

We build, maintain, and evolve plaid.com — the front door to every product launch, campaign, and industry narrative Plaid puts into the world.

Thirteen people. Five disciplines. One website that touches every part of how Plaid shows up in the world. The core web team is where design, content, engineering, growth, and program management come together — and where plaid.com gets made.

We treat the website like a product: we measure it, experiment on it, and hold it to the same bar as anything else Plaid ships. And we don't do it alone — the web team collaborates constantly with brand, demand gen, product marketing, sales, and engineering partners across Plaid. These are the core builders, but the work is never just us.
